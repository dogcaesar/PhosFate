PhosFate Profiler

PhosFate Profiler allows to analyze quantitative phosphoproteomic data, in order to biologically interpret the molecular responses after perturbation. PhosFate Profiler integrates the provided quantifications with known regulatory data, summarizing different levels of molecular regulation, such as changes in the kinase activities or protein complex regulation. The server also compares the signaling response with other published phosphoproteomes providing some clues on the specificity of the response. The data submitted to PhosFate Profiler remains private as all the analysis is performed in your local computer.
